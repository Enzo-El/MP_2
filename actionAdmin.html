ACTION TAKEN

PLEASE BE GUIDED ACCORDINGLY:

verified

Please take note that you should only choose the appropriate option based on the actual action taken related to the incident reported.

  1. Pending - this is the "default" status of the all the reports/blotters submitted first hand. This status appears on both the user's page and the admin's page. You may opt not to change this status unless otherwise you have taken an action, either physical or virtual to initially handle the complaint.

  2. For Verification - this is "first stage" of action the admin must take with the help of an officer or verification team. This is the actual verification of the reported details in relation to the complaint. It is crucial to note the email address and the contact number of the complainant for proper coordination and communication.

  3. Under Investigation - this is "second stage" of action the admin must take in order to identify the root cause and the impact of the reported blotter/complaint. This is the data/evidence gathering stage, it is important to closely coordinate with the complainant the data needed (such as sensitive photo, videos, cctv and audio recordings) that will help in solving the case.

  4. For Mediation - this is "third stage" of action the admin must take in order to try to resolve the reported incident by a closed-door meeting and conversation of the complainant (affected person) and the respondent (perpetuator) with the help of a "Mediating Officer"

  5. Resolved - this is the "final act" once the blotter/incident report has been duly solved (at the Barangay-Level) or escalated to higher courts for trial and jury's verdict.
